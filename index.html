I am a software engineer with a passion for travel and gaming with a background in Finance. I worked as a Staff Accountant and eventually moved into Accounts Receivable Analyst role. These roles ultimately aided me in honing my analytical and critical thinking abilities, but I realized that finance wasn't a career path that I would enjoy pursuing for the rest of my life. I had had thoughts of a career in software engineering since graduating from college, and I was lucky enough to explore it briefly in finance.

I attended a JavaScript workshop run by Launch Academy to see if coding was really for me. During the class we built out a front-end interactive Rock-Paper-Scissors web app and I was hooked. I knew then, that a career in software engineering was the direction that I needed to take. Ultimately I would love to be able to utilize my past analytical skills and current acquired skills as a software engineer to work in the gaming and entertainment industry
